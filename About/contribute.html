Requirements

There are a few requirements that must be fufilled if you wish to put your research papers on this site.

  1. Works sited: all sources must be properly sited.
  2. Peer review: a teacher or peer must have read and accepted your research paper.
  3. Agreement: Read and agreeded to the Terms and Conditions and privacy policy. IT IS OF UTMOST IMPORTANCE THAT YOU READ AND AGREE TO THE TERMS AND CONDITIONS AND PRIVACY POLICY.
